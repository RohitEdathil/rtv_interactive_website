about the hobby

Who, what, and why behind rtv.interactive.

Part-Time Passion

rtv.interactive is a personal side hobby born out of a love for clean user interfaces, game mechanics, and minimalist utility apps.

By day, I write standard software. By night, I experiment with utility apps, grid puzzles, and board games. The goal is to build things that run fast, look clean, and respect the user's privacy and intelligence.

Zero Bloat Philosophy

Many mobile apps and games today are filled with aggressive tracking, screen-wide ads, and heavy cloud dependencies. rtv.interactive tools aim to be the opposite: local-first, minimal permissions, and immediate functionality.

Hobby Details

  • Focus: Minimalist UI, Local Utilities, Puzzles
  • Ethics: Zero trackers, offline play
  • Status: Active side-hobby
